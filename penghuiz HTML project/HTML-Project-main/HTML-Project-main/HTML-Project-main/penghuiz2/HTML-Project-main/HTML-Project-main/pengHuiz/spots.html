Good things about NYC

Good things Why?
Transportation It's very easy to travel in NYC. Most places are walkable and the state provides pretty decent public transportation
It's a city You can do a lot of stuff in a city, unlike the suburbs.
Diversity NYC has a lot of people that come from different places. You can experience all sorts of cultures here

Bad things about NY

Bad things Why?
Rats It's pretty common to see rats in NY, especially in the subway stations. The rats are dead half the time and it's quite unappealing.
Cost NY is one of the most expensive cities in the world. The cost of living here is higher than a majority of the country, and things don't seem to be looking the best with the rise of inflation.
Druggies NY has a lot of drug addicts. They may try attacking or begging you for money.

Even though NY has its bads, I think I'll be staying here for a while.
